Architecture

The Blue Citadel rises from the Mediterranean shoreline as both fortress and sanctuary — its arched colonnades speaking a language older than time, yet tuned to the rhythms of the modern soul.

Every stone, every shadow, every reflection in the still marina waters was considered as an act of devotion to those who seek more than comfort — who seek transformation.
